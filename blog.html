Essential Tax Deductions Every Small Business Should Know

February 18, 2025

Small business owners can significantly reduce their tax liability through various deductions. Key deductible expenses include office supplies, furniture, and equipment, as well as business vehicle expenses tracked through mileage logs or actual costs. Professional services like legal and accounting fees are also deductible, along with home office expenses for those who qualify. Proper documentation is crucial, requiring detailed receipts, statements, and logs. To maximize deductions, businesses should implement reliable expense tracking systems, use accounting software, and stay updated with IRS guidelines. Consulting tax professionals can help navigate complex deduction scenarios effectively.
